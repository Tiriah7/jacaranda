Looking for a break in the heart of Lorraine? For your business trips?


Then look no further! Located just 30 minutes from Nancy and 1 hour from Strasbourg, domain Jacaranda de Banaudon welcomes you every day from Tuesday to Saturday and offers you an ideal stay in the historic town of Lunéville. Our mansion dates back to 1750 and was once the home of the Minotiers family. Located in a privileged location, in the town centre and just opposite the Town Hall, Jacaranda de Banaudon offers you possibility to lodge in its prestigious three guest rooms and two charming guest houses. You will have access to a garden of more than 1000 square metres and a covered swimming pool (open according to prefectural agreement).



Live the history at Lunéville Castle


Lunéville, rich in history, is home to the majestic Castle of King Stanislas. Immerse yourself in the world of royalty with a visit to this emblematic monument of ancient times. You'll be amazed by its sumptuous architecture and the breathtaking bosquet gardens.
